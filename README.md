FOREST VOID - A University Of Bristol Project
---------------------------------------------

#### A VR Horror Game developed in Unity, using:
- #####   Oculus Rift V1 
- #####   Oculus Touch
- #####   Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Heart Rate Monitor

<p align="center">
<b  style="font-size:50%;">As a search and rescue officer, you have received a distress call from a female located in the depths of an inhabited forest. However, she describes an entity that skulks the forest. </b>
</p> 

<p align="center" style="font-size:10px;">
<b>It is your mission to retrieve her. </b>
</p>
